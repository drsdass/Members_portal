One Health Holdings (First Bio Lab, First Bio Genetics, First Bio Lab of Illinois, AIM Laboratories, AMICO Dx, STAT Labs and Enviro Labs) Patient & Provider Portal

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

At One Health Holdings, your privacy and the confidentiality of your health information are our top priorities. This Privacy Disclosure outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and health-related data through our online portal.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you use our portal:

Patients:

  • Full name, date of birth, and last 4 digits of SSN
  • Contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Lab results and diagnostic data
  • Portal activity (e.g., login time, pages accessed)

Providers:

  • Name, NPI number, credentials
  • Username and password
  • Patient search history and access logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide access to diagnostic test results
  • Authenticate users securely
  • Enable communication between patients and providers
  • Maintain audit trails for compliance and accountability
  • Improve portal functionality and security

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Your information may only be shared:

  • With healthcare providers involved in your care
  • With laboratories under One Health Holdings for diagnostic purposes
  • When required by law or in response to legal process
  • With third-party vendors (e.g., cloud services) under strict confidentiality and compliance agreements

4. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including:

  • Data encryption (in transit and at rest)
  • Role-based access controls (RBAC)
  • Audit logging
  • Secure hosting and network protections
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for provider access

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct or update inaccurate data
  • Request a record of disclosures
  • Withdraw access or close your account (subject to legal retention rules)

6. Retention Policy

We retain patient and provider data in accordance with applicable federal and state laws, including HIPAA requirements, and our internal compliance policy.
